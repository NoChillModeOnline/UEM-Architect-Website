Privacy & Cookie Policy

How UEM Architect collects, uses, and protects your personal data — and how we use cookies on this website.

Last updated: April 2026

1. Who We Are

UEM Architect Consulting ("UEM Architect," "we," "us," or "our") is a Unified Endpoint Management consulting firm based in Odessa, Florida, USA. We provide endpoint management strategy, platform deployment, managed operations, and related IT consulting services to organizations worldwide.

For questions about this policy or your personal data, contact us at: contact@uemarchitect.org

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data only in the following circumstances:

  • Contact form submissions: When you submit the contact form on our website, we collect your name, work email address, company name, and the message you provide. This data is transmitted to and stored by Formspree, our third-party form processing service.
  • Newsletter sign-ups: If you subscribe to our newsletter via the footer form, we collect your email address, also processed via Formspree.
  • Booking requests: If you schedule a meeting via our booking link (zeeg.me), that service collects data in accordance with Zeeg's Privacy Policy.

We do not collect data through analytics tools, advertising pixels, or any passive tracking mechanisms. We have no visibility into who visits our website unless you actively contact us.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data you submit exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Responding to your inquiry or consultation request
  • Sending the newsletter content you subscribed to (newsletter signups only)
  • Scheduling and conducting meetings you book with our team

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR): Responding to business inquiries you initiate through our contact form.
  • Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR): Newsletter subscriptions, where you actively opt in by submitting your email address.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

This website uses a small number of cookies and browser local storage entries. We categorise them as follows:

Essential Cookies & Storage

These are strictly necessary for the site to function. They cannot be disabled without affecting core functionality.

  • uema_cookie_consent — Stored in browser localStorage. Saves your cookie consent preference (accepted or essential-only) so you are not shown the banner on every visit. Persists until you clear your browser data. No expiry is set.
  • Formspree cookies — When you submit a contact or newsletter form, Formspree may set cookies used for spam prevention and security (CSRF protection). These are essential to the form submission process. See Formspree's Privacy Policy for full details.

Non-Essential Cookies

This website currently sets no advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, LinkedIn Insight Tag, or any equivalent tool. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated and consent re-obtained before any non-essential cookies are placed.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Formspree — Form processing and email delivery. Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Web fonts loaded from Google's CDN. Google may collect your IP address when fonts are served. Google Privacy Policy
  • Zeeg — Meeting scheduling (linked from our Contact page). Privacy Policy
  • LinkedIn — Linked from our footer and contact page. Any data you provide on LinkedIn is governed by LinkedIn's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

Contact form and newsletter submissions are retained by Formspree in accordance with their data retention policies. We retain copies of email correspondence for as long as they are relevant to an active business relationship, and no longer than three years after the last interaction unless a legal obligation requires a different retention period.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction — Request that we limit how we process your data.
  • Right to data portability — Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — If processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@uemarchitect.org. We will respond within 30 days. If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can change or withdraw your cookie consent at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage for this site (the key is uema_cookie_consent) — the consent banner will reappear on your next visit. You can also use your browser's built-in settings to block or delete cookies at any time. Note that blocking all cookies may affect the ability to submit forms on this site.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website is directed at business professionals and is not intended for use by individuals under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy & Cookie Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If we make material changes that affect how we use your personal data, we will take reasonable steps to notify you.
