Why UEM Architect Consulting?

Most consultants show up with a vendor recommendation already in mind. We show up with questions — because the right solution for your organization depends entirely on how your people work, what you're protecting, and where you're headed. Here's what that looks like in practice.

What Makes Us Different

We're a small, senior team — which means every engagement gets our full attention, not a junior resource following a script. These are the principles that guide our work.

Workflow-First Approach

Before we touch a policy or configure a platform, we learn how your teams actually work. We map IT decisions to real workflows — so enrollments, compliance rules, and access policies align with day-to-day operations, not just textbook best practices. The result is a UEM environment your people will actually use correctly.

Deep, Certified Platform Expertise

We hold active certifications across Omnissa (Workspace ONE), Microsoft Intune, Jamf, Microsoft Entra ID, and more — including our status as a proud Omnissa Silver Partner and Solution Reseller. That means we don't just advise; we understand the platforms at an architectural level and can connect the dots across your entire endpoint and identity stack.

Documentation You'll Actually Use

Every engagement ends with clear, human-readable documentation — architecture diagrams, policy runbooks, configuration rationale, and handoff guides. When your next hire joins or an auditor asks questions, you'll have the answers ready. No more reverse-engineering configurations after the fact.

Our Commitments to Every Client

These aren't aspirational values — they're operating standards we hold ourselves to on every project, regardless of your size or budget.

Vendor-Certified, Vendor-Neutral Advice

We're certified across multiple platforms, which gives us a genuine comparison point — not a sales pitch. When we recommend a platform or configuration, it's because it fits your environment, your team, and your budget. If your current tool can do the job with better configuration, we'll tell you that too.

Clear, Consistent Communication

You'll always know where things stand. Every engagement includes regular status updates, a named point of contact, and plain-English explanations — no jargon, no surprises. If something changes mid-project, you hear it from us directly, not through a status report three weeks later.

Designed for Where You're Going

We plan for what's next — headcount growth, platform migrations, compliance requirements, hybrid work expansion. Every recommendation is built with scalability in mind so you're not redoing this work in 18 months. We help you build an endpoint environment that grows with your organization, not one that boxes it in.

Omnissa Silver Partner & Solution Reseller

Our Omnissa Silver Partner status is not self-declared — it's a designation awarded by Omnissa to firms that have met verified standards in technical training, platform certifications, and real-world deployment experience. It means our team has been evaluated and recognized by the vendor itself as qualified to design, deploy, and manage Workspace ONE environments professionally.

In practical terms, this gives our clients access to deeper support resources. When issues arise, we have direct access to Omnissa's partner technical channels — faster escalation paths than a standard support ticket. We also receive early visibility into platform roadmap updates, which means we can help you make licensing and architecture decisions with forward-looking context, not just what's available today.

As a certified Solution Reseller, we're authorized to procure and license Omnissa products directly on your behalf. There's no need to involve a separate Omnissa sales representative or navigate unfamiliar vendor contracts. We advise you on the right licensing structure for your organization's size and use case, handle the procurement, and support renewals and scope changes as your needs evolve — all within one trusted engagement.

You get a team that's been vetted by Omnissa, supported by Omnissa, and authorized to license on your behalf — without any of the overhead of managing those relationships separately.
