Our Services

Six focused service areas covering the full endpoint management lifecycle — from discovering what's broken to deploying what works to keeping it running. As an Omnissa Silver Partner and Solution Reseller, we also procure and license Workspace ONE on your behalf, making us a single point of contact for expertise and platform access.

What We Deliver

Each service is designed to solve a specific phase of the endpoint management challenge — and they're built to work together as a cohesive strategy, not isolated projects.

Endpoint Assessment & Roadmap

Before making any changes, we build a complete picture of where you are today — your platforms, policies, configurations, coverage gaps, and security posture. The result is a clear, prioritized roadmap that tells you what to fix first, what to build next, and what to defer, backed by data and documented rationale.

What's Included

  • Current-state platform and policy audit across all managed device types
  • Gap analysis against security frameworks (Zero Trust, CIS, NIST, or your own standards)
  • Risk scoring for identified gaps — critical, high, medium, and low priority
  • Prioritized remediation roadmap with phased implementation recommendations
  • Executive summary and technical detail documentation for IT leadership
  • Vendor-neutral platform recommendation (if a migration or addition is warranted)

Platform Deployment & Migration

Whether you're deploying a new UEM platform from scratch or migrating away from a legacy solution, we manage the full technical scope — design, configuration, testing, and go-live — while minimizing disruption to your users and maintaining continuity of policy enforcement throughout the transition.

What's Included

  • Platform architecture design tailored to your organizational structure and device types
  • Tenant or on-premise environment buildout with enrollment profiles and configuration policies
  • Application deployment framework — required apps, optional apps, and VPP/ABM/AOSP integration
  • Migration runbook covering device re-enrollment, data continuity, and rollback procedures
  • User acceptance testing (UAT) and pilot group validation before full rollout
  • Complete configuration documentation and change log delivered at project close

Managed UEM Operations

Your UEM platform requires ongoing attention to stay effective — policy updates, OS release management, enrollment support, compliance monitoring, and more. Our managed operations service gives you a dedicated UEM team without the cost of a full-time hire, keeping your environment healthy and your IT team focused on higher-value work.

What's Included

  • Day-to-day platform management — enrollment troubleshooting, policy changes, and device remediation
  • OS and app update testing and staged deployment coordination
  • Compliance posture monitoring with proactive alerts for non-compliant or unmanaged devices
  • Regular platform health reviews and configuration drift correction
  • Monthly reporting on device coverage, compliance rates, and open issues
  • Documentation updates as changes are made — always current, always yours

Security & Zero Trust

Endpoint security isn't a checkbox — it's an architecture. We help organizations design and implement Zero Trust frameworks that treat device health, user identity, and network location as first-class signals for access decisions. The result is a security posture that's enforced at the endpoint layer, not just at the perimeter.

What's Included

  • Zero Trust architecture design aligned to your identity provider, UEM platform, and network
  • Conditional access policy design and implementation (Entra ID, Okta, or equivalent)
  • Device compliance policy buildout — encryption, patch level, threat detection, and OS version gating
  • CIS Benchmark or STIG baseline configuration for Windows, macOS, iOS, and Android
  • Data loss prevention (DLP) policy design and implementation
  • Security posture documentation suitable for audit, compliance review, or executive reporting

Identity & Access Management

Identity is the control plane of the modern endpoint environment. We help organizations build a unified identity foundation that connects device management, application access, and security policy into a single coherent model — reducing credential sprawl, improving user experience, and tightening access governance across the board.

What's Included

  • Identity provider assessment and configuration (Entra ID, Okta, or on-premise AD hybrid)
  • Single sign-on (SSO) implementation for managed applications and cloud services
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) rollout with phased enforcement and user communication planning
  • User lifecycle automation — provisioning, role changes, and deprovisioning workflows
  • Privileged access review and least-privilege policy design
  • Identity-device binding to ensure access decisions account for both user and device posture

Training & Enablement

A well-built UEM environment only stays healthy if the people managing it understand how it works and why it was built the way it was. Our training and enablement service ensures your IT team can operate, troubleshoot, and evolve the environment confidently — and that end users know what to expect from their managed devices.

What's Included

  • Admin training workshops tailored to your specific platform(s) and configuration
  • Standard operating procedures (SOPs) for common admin tasks — enrollment, policy changes, incident response
  • Runbooks for recurring operational workflows your team will own after engagement close
  • End-user communication templates and onboarding guides for new device rollouts
  • Train-the-trainer sessions for organizations with distributed IT teams or MSP partners
  • Post-engagement support period with access to our team for questions and clarification
