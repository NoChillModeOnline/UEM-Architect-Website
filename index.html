Proud Omnissa Silver Partner & Solution Reseller

UEM Architect Consulting holds Silver Partner status with Omnissa — the company behind Workspace ONE, one of the most widely deployed Unified Endpoint Management platforms in the world. Silver Partner status isn't self-declared; it's awarded by Omnissa to firms that have met verified standards across technical training, platform certifications, and real-world deployment experience. It means we've been evaluated by the vendor and recognized as a qualified, trusted partner.

What that means in practice: our team has completed Omnissa's official training programs, earned platform certifications, and demonstrated hands-on experience managing Workspace ONE environments across industries. We have access to Omnissa's partner support channels and technical resources — which means faster escalation paths and earlier visibility into platform updates when it matters for your project.

Our Solution Reseller designation goes one step further. We are authorized to procure and license Omnissa products directly on your behalf. That means you don't need a separate relationship with Omnissa's sales team. We can advise you on the right licensing tier for your environment, handle the procurement process, and support renewals and upgrades over time — all as part of a single engagement with one team you already trust.

Strategy, implementation, and licensing — all from one vetted, certified partner. No vendor runaround. No separate sales calls. Just expertise and execution.
